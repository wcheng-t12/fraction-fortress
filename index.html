FRACTION FORTRESS

Tower Defense · Math Strategy

📖 How to Play

🎯 Goal

Stop enemy waves from reaching your fortress! Place fraction towers, merge them to upgrade, and survive all 10 rounds!

⚔️ Placing & Editing Towers

  • Select a fraction card, then click an empty slot (grey + circle on the path) to place it.
  • To swap or remove a tower you already placed — just click directly on that tower! A menu pops up letting you replace it with any card from your hand, or remove it entirely (the fraction goes back to your hand).
  • Bigger fraction = stronger tower, wider range, and faster fire rate!

➕ Merging (Upgrading)

  • Select two cards → click "Merge" to add them using proper fraction addition (LCD method).
  • Example: 12+ 13= 56
  • Keep merging to make powerful improper fractions like 74!

⭐ Whole Number Bonus!

  • When merging creates a whole number (1/1 → 1, 2/1 → 2, 3/1 → 3…), you earn a massive bonus!
  • 1 = +200 pts  |  2 = +400 pts  |  3 = +600 pts
  • The bonus also triggers when you place or swap a whole number card onto a slot!
  • Whole number towers deal triple damage, have wider range, and shoot much faster — golden glow shows they're special!

❤️ Lives & Scoring

  • You have 5 lives. Each enemy reaching the fortress costs 1 life.
  • Earn points for kills — bigger fraction towers earn more per kill.
  • Completing a round earns a round clear bonus.
  • Each round: more enemies, higher HP, faster speed.
