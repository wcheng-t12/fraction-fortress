FRACTION FORTRESS

Tower Defense · Math Strategy

⚔️

Welcome, Commander!

Defend your fortress from enemy waves by placing fraction towers along the path. Merge cards to upgrade and unleash Whole Number power!

10 rounds · survive & score big

🎯

Place & Edit Towers

Select a fraction card from your hand, then tap an empty + slot on the path. To swap or remove a placed tower, just click directly on it — a menu pops up!

Bigger fraction = wider range + faster fire rate

➕

Merge & Upgrade

Select two cards → click Merge. They add using LCD:

12 + 13 = 56

Merge to build powerful improper fractions!

⭐

Whole Number Bonuses!

When merging creates a whole number (like 1, 2, 3…), you earn a massive bonus!

1 = +200 pts  ·  2 = +400 pts  ·  3 = +600 pts

✨ Whole towers deal triple damage & glow gold!

❤️

Lives & Scoring

Start with 5 lives. Each enemy that reaches the fortress costs 1 life.

Earn points for each kill — bigger fractions give more points. Clear a round for a bonus!

Each round: more enemies, higher HP, faster speed.
